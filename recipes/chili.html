Chili

Description

This is a stovetop recipe for chili that uses ground beef, but you can also use other meats such as ground turkey or even chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1.5lbs ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 small onion (diced)
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can diced tomato
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp Paprika
  • 2 tbsp Chili powder
  • 1 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Onion powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add ground beef and onion to large stockpot. Cook on medium heat until beef is browned and onion is cooked through.
  2. Add beans, tomato, and seasonings
  3. Add 1/2cup of water if needed
  4. Decrease heat to a simmer
  5. Cook for 20+ min until all flavors meld
