Quinoa

Description

This is a simple recipe to prepare quinoa in a rice cooker.

Ingredients

  • 1lbs dry quinoa
  • 4.5cups water
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • Optional: salt, garlic powder, bay leaf

Steps

  1. Rinse and drain quinoa
  2. Put quinoa in rice cooker and fill with 4.5cups of water
  3. Add olive oil
  4. Add any desired seasonings
  5. Cook in rice cooker with regular settings for white rice
