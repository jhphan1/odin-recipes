Hamburger

Description

This is a simple recipe for the classic hamburger with the option to add cheese and other toppings!

Ingredients

  • 3lbs ground beef (80/20)
  • 10 sesame buns
  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 3 onions
  • Optional: 1 small pack of sliced American cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into 1/4lbs patties
  2. Season patties generously with salt and pepper
  3. Grill on med high heat until charred on each side
  4. While meat is grilling, slice tomatoes and onions, and separate lettuce leaves
  5. Optional: Add cheese to patties on grill after flipping
  6. Assemble cooked patties and veggies onto patties
